Garnier Hair Color Logic Diagram

This diagram explains how the Garnier Hair Color predictor determines your perfect blonde shade based on your zodiac sign, job, personality type, and toxic trait.

Your Sign

Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, Pisces

Your Job

Creative, Corporate, Medical, Educator, Tech, Service

Your Type

Introvert, Extrovert, Ambivert, Logical, Emotional, Intuitive

Your Toxic Trait

Perfectionist, Overthinker, Procrastinator, People Pleaser, Impulsive, Stubborn

↓

Sign Points

  • Fire Signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): Salted Caramelo (+3)
  • Air Signs (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): Tres Leches (+3)
  • Earth Signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): Coconut Flan (+3)
  • Water Signs (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): Coconut Flan (+2), Tres Leches (+1)

Job Points

  • Creative/Tech: Salted Caramelo (+2), Tres Leches (+1)
  • Corporate/Medical: Tres Leches (+3)
  • Educator/Service: Coconut Flan (+3)

Type Points

  • Extrovert/Intuitive: Salted Caramelo (+3)
  • Logical/Ambivert: Tres Leches (+3)
  • Introvert/Emotional: Coconut Flan (+3)

Toxic Trait Points

  • Impulsive/Stubborn: Salted Caramelo (+3)
  • Perfectionist/Overthinker: Tres Leches (+3)
  • Procrastinator/People Pleaser: Coconut Flan (+3)
↓

Salted Caramelo

Warm, golden-brown shade with caramel tones. Perfect for those with bold, dynamic personalities.

  • Powerhouse
  • Rule Breaker
  • Game Changer
  • Firecracker
  • Party Animal

Tres Leches

Light, creamy blonde with subtle warm undertones. Ideal for those with intellectual, ambitious personalities.

  • Brainiac Baddie
  • Savage Thinker
  • Mastermind
  • Boss Lady
  • Visionary Vixen

Coconut Flan

Cool, light blonde with hints of golden tones. Perfect for those with balanced, harmonious personalities.

  • Chill Queen
  • Charmer Extraordinaire
  • Rock Solid Bae
  • Watchdog
  • Socialite
  • Globetrotter
↓

Final Determination

The hair color with the highest total points is selected. If there is a tie, one of the tied colors is selected at random.

Then, a random personality type is chosen from that hair color group to complete your result.

Maximum Points Per Hair Color

  • Salted Caramelo: 11 points
  • Tres Leches: 10 points
  • Coconut Flan: 12 points
