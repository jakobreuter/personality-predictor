Personality Determination

The personality type with the highest point total is selected. If there is a tie, one of the tied personalities is chosen at random.

Salted Caramelo Personalities

  • Powerhouse: A Powerhouse is a total boss who runs the game with a lethal combo of brains and style. She doesn't ask for permission, she takes what she wants and makes it look effortless.
  • Rule Breaker: A Rule Breaker is the one who laughs in the face of expectations. Bold, daring, and never afraid to stir things up, she's got the guts to turn the world upside down and make it her playground.
  • Game Changer: A Game Changer doesn't follow trends, she sets them. Fearless and unstoppable, she's here to flip the script and show everyone how it's done, leaving a trail of jaw-dropped admirers in her wake.
  • Firecracker: A Firecracker is all spark, no filter. Her energy is explosive, her presence undeniable, and she's got a knack for turning even the most boring situations into unforgettable moments.
  • Party Animal: A Party Animal is the life of the scene, bringing the chaos and the good vibes wherever she goes. If there's a party, you'll find her at the center, making sure the fun never stops.

Tres Leches Personalities

  • Brainiac Baddie: A Brainiac Baddie is a genius wrapped in confidence. She breaks all the rules, redefines the game, and leaves everyone wondering how she's so smart and so fierce.
  • Savage Thinker: A Savage Thinker loves a good debate and isn't afraid to tear apart your arguments. Quick-witted and sharp, she wins every intellectual battle with a side of shade and a sprinkle of sass.
  • Mastermind: A Mastermind doesn't just create; she dominates. Her genius is matched only by her relentless ambition, and she's always three steps ahead, making moves while everyone else is still trying to catch up.
  • Boss Lady: A Boss Lady isn't just a leader; she's the one everyone's striving to be. She takes control with precision, runs circles around competition, and handles everything with power, poise, and plenty of attitude.
  • Visionary Vixen: A Visionary Vixen doesn't just dream, she makes her vision reality. Unstoppable and determined, she's got big ideas and an even bigger drive to show everyone who's in charge.

Coconut Flan Personalities

  • Chill Queen: A Chill Queen keeps it cool no matter the drama. She brings calm to chaos and handles everything with a relaxed confidence that makes others wish they could be as effortlessly chill as she is.
  • Charmer Extraordinaire: A Charmer Extraordinaire has everyone eating out of her hand. With just the right amount of flirt and finesse, she can talk circles around anyone and leave them wanting more.
  • Rock Solid Bae: A Rock Solid Bae is steady, unshakable, and keeps it real at all times. When she says she's got you, she has you, and you can count on her to handle anything life throws her way with zero drama.
  • Watchdog: A Watchdog has your back, no questions asked. Loyal to the core and fiercely protective, she doesn't let anyone mess with her people—she's a force to be reckoned with, and she's not afraid to fight back.
  • Socialite: A Socialite is always at the center of the action, effortlessly turning every event into the hottest spot in town. Charismatic and sharp, she knows how to work a room and make everything feel like a VIP affair.
  • Globetrotter: A Globetrotter is a wanderer with a taste for the extraordinary. Always booking her next adventure, she lives for exploring the world and doing it in style, making every trip look like a mini runway show.
